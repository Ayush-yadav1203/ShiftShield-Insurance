# 🚀 ShiftShield – AI Insurance for Gig Workers

## 🧑‍💼 Persona
Food Delivery Partner (Zomato/Swiggy)

---

## ❗ Problem
Delivery partners lose income during peak earning hours due to disruptions like rain, traffic, or app issues. These disruptions reduce their earnings significantly and there is no system to compensate them.

---

## 💡 Solution
We propose ShiftShield – a time-slot based AI insurance platform that protects gig workers during their highest earning hours (lunch and dinner shifts).

Instead of generic daily coverage, our system insures specific working shifts and uses AI to predict expected earnings. If disruptions reduce income during these critical hours, payouts are automatically triggered.

---

## ⚙️ Workflow
1. User selects working shifts (Lunch/Dinner)
2. AI predicts expected earnings
3. System monitors weather, traffic, and order volume
4. If income drops → automatic claim triggered
5. Instant payout processed

---

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model
Lunch Plan: ₹25/week  
Dinner Plan: ₹40/week  
Full Coverage: ₹60/week  

---

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers
- Rain + Low Orders
- Traffic Congestion
- App Downtime
- Sudden Drop in Requests
